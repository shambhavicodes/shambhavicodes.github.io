Shambhavi Mishra

Hi! I’m Shambhavi Mishra, a PhD student at École de technologie supérieure ÉTS, working under the guidance of Prof. Jose Dolz and Prof. Marco Pedersoli. My research focuses on advancing Foundation Models for tackling challenges in label-scarce learning scenarios, with a keen emphasis on improving the reliability and calibration of predictions in these contexts.

Beyond academia, I’ve had the privilege of contributing to cutting-edge industry projects. During my time at companies like FrandAvenue, NeuroSensum, I worked on diverse applications, including automated SEP Claim Chart generation using LLaMA, text analytics on multilingual data, and patent data analysis. These experiences have shaped my approach to problem-solving, blending research depth with real-world applications.

My passion extends to community building and knowledge sharing. I founded Computer Vision Talks, an initiative aimed at fostering research discussions and collaborations among students and professionals. I’ve also organized machine learning workshopsfor underprivileged communities during my undergrad, combining my love for teaching with my drive to make AI accessible to all.

Feel free to explore my work, projects, and ideas. I’m always excited about new collaborations and meaningful discussions—let’s connect!
